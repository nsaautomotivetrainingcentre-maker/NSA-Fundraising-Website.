Our Mission: Transforming Lives Through Automotive Education

The NSA Automotive Training Centre/Academy is a **non-profit organization** dedicated to providing life-changing opportunities for passionate, less fortunate youth across South Africa and neighboring countries.

We offer **state-of-the-art education** and ensure **guaranteed permanent employment after graduation**, with an ambitious goal to **employ over 4,000 individuals** in 3-5 years, building stronger communities.

This page serves as a vital **financial backbone** to support these initiatives through scholarships, bursaries, and various fundraising efforts.

Scholarships & Bursaries: Investing in Talent

Our scholarship and bursary programs are designed to remove financial barriers, ensuring that deserving youth, regardless of their economic background, can access our high-quality automotive training and secure a promising future.

Empowering Access

Scholarships cover tuition, materials, and living expenses, ensuring students can focus entirely on their studies and practical training.

Future-Proofing Careers

By funding a scholarship, you directly contribute to a student's journey from training to guaranteed permanent employment.
